15-20 Min Simple Exercises

For flexibility, increased motion, and body fit.

1. Neck & Shoulder Rolls (3 mins)

Slowly roll your neck in circles. Roll shoulders forward and backward to release tension.

2. Arm Circles & Reaches (3 mins)

Wide arm circles to improve shoulder mobility. Reach high and try to touch your toes.

3. Torso Twists & Side Bends (4 mins)

Stand with feet wide. Twist your upper body side to side. Bend laterally to stretch obliques.

4. Hip Circles & Leg Swings (4 mins)

Circle your hips like a hula hoop. Hold a wall and swing each leg forward, backward, and side to side.

5. Calm Breathing & Stretching (4 mins)

Deep inhales and exhales. Finish with a full body stretch reaching upwards.

6. Light Walk in Place (2 mins)

Gentle march in place to bring the heart rate back down and loosen the legs.
